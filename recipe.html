Morning Pour Over

Equipment

coffee_maker

Chemex

6-cup brewer

settings

Baratza Encore

Setting: 21

Ingredients

coffee

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

18g • Light Roast

water_drop

Filtered Water

300ml • 95°C

Steps

1

Rinse & Preheat

Place filter and rinse with hot water. This preheats the Chemex and removes paper taste.

30 sec
2

Bloom

Add 36g of water (2x coffee weight). Gently stir to ensure all grounds are saturated.

30 sec
3

First Pour

Slowly pour water in circular motion until reaching 150ml total.

1 min
4

Second Pour

Continue pouring to 300ml total. Maintain water level in dripper.

2 min

Notes

Best served immediately. Adjust grind size if brew time is off - finer if too fast, coarser if too slow. Target total brew time: 4 minutes.
